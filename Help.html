Learn how our Crowdsourced Civic Issue Reporting & Resolution System empowers citizens and authorities to collaborate for better cities.

Our Idea

We propose a Crowdsourced Civic Issue Reporting & Resolution System where citizens can easily report issues with photos and location, track real-time status, and upvote common problems. Authorities get a dashboard to assign, update, and close complaints with transparency.

By focusing on simplicity, multilingual support, accountability loops, and community validation, the system builds trust and ensures higher adoption—directly improving urban governance under the Smart Cities mission.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a civic issue?

You can report issues directly from the home page by clicking on “Report an Issue”. Use your phone’s camera and location for quick submissions.

Can I track the status of my complaint?

Yes. Every complaint is assigned a unique ID. You can track progress and see updates in real-time on your dashboard.

What is upvoting of issues?

If multiple citizens face the same problem, they can upvote it. This helps authorities prioritize the most urgent and common issues.

How does this ensure transparency?

All actions—assignment, progress updates, and closures—are visible to citizens. This accountability loop ensures trust in the system.

Is the system multilingual?

Yes, the platform supports multiple languages so that citizens from all regions can easily participate.
